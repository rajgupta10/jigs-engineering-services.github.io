About Us

Founded in 2015 with roots in the Chemical Process Industries, Jigs Engineering Services has swiftly ascended to a prominent position in the industry. Driven by the foresight of its founder and the collective dedication of its believers, the company stands as a leader today.

As a premier solutions provider, Jigs Engineering Services offers a comprehensive range of services including Flow and Terminal Automation, Process Instrumentation, and Fire Alarm systems among others. Catering to diverse sectors such as Cement, Steel, Oil & Gas, and more, we specialize in delivering tailored solutions to meet industry-specific needs.

With a robust presence in major Indian cities, our reach extends across markets. Bolstered by a state-of-the-art fabrication team and a talented workforce, Jigs Engineering Services is a formidable entity in the Indian Industrial landscape. Our commitment to technological advancement and collaboration with industry leaders reinforces our avant-garde position in the Process Industry.
